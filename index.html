ENTERPRISE CLIENTS

Product firms of all sizes come to us to unload some of their design and engineering activities.

  • Do you need additional engineering staff during high-demand periods?
  • Do you require specialized engineering expertise currently unavailable at your organization?
  • Do you require on-demand help for product design, engineering or manufacturing introduction?
  • Do you need to free up your key engineers to focus on other priorities?

Dextrous Design Engineering is committed to helping your company succeed in:

  • Meeting demanding deadlines
  • Reaching product cost targets
  • Differentiating your product from the competition
  • Incorporating innovative mechanical designs
  • Achieving aesthetic qualities without compromising functionality
  • Finding cost-saving and manufacturable redesigns
  • Making patentable products to strengthen your portfolio

We deliver high quality work on time and the assurance that your projects are in good hands with us as your partner.
